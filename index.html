what is an escape room?

An escape room is a popular activity which can be done by several participants. Locked in a room symbolizing a closed place (a castle, a pirate ship, an Egyptian pyramid, a prison, a distant planet, etc.), the participants must work together to investigate and solve the puzzles that will allow them to escape in a limited time.Reflection, cooperation, imagination and fun, the Escape Room allows everyone to share an amazing and unforgettable moment.
